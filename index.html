I am Matteo, I am 27-years-old, and lately I have been thinking to start a business. I like art, I like going to exhibitions and my favourite artist is Munch. I graduated in theoretical physics, then I did some research in cosmology, and then in neuroscience. Now I'm working in R&D in the space telecommunication sector. I have some experience (but not as much as I would love to) in ML/AI. I keep myself updated through newsletter (I suggest TDLR AI), and I code stuff on my own when I have an idea. Be aware that, although I code a lot, I am not a code expert! Probably because of my love for art, I also like posters and contemporary prints. My curiosity (a business soon?) is to see whether Artificial Intelligence can generate posters/prints in the style of Drool Art (), Santo manifesto (), Desenio (), etc., of course cutting down the costs! Although there are a lot of data (loads of posters online), I guess this won't be easy as it seems. That's why I need you! I do believe that 1) sharing this experience together is better than building alone 2) you are better than me at AI/ML! So far, the idea I got is to sell posters such that they are (really) affordable to everyone. A rough business idea may be print on demand (POD) to cut down personal costs, and to find a way to sell poster with a low margin (this way we can set a lower price to customers, say 5-6 Euros for an A4 poster). If the model works, we will cut down costs related to artists, and essentially we can generate our posters for free and sell them with a low margin. The other idea is that we may sell our software (to generate posters) to major competitors for a much higher price. Of course, if it works.. I know that 1) there are a lot of competitors / people already selling posters / arts 2) the idea may be a little bit dumb. However, re. 1) as far as I know there's no ai model working at the desired quality (link above for ref.) 2) usually the idea when you start a business counts until a certain extent. I guess the most important think is the desire and excitement and commitment to make something together/start a company; the idea will likely change over time if we are not stupid stupid. Also, I know that people can buy art because they like the real (human) work behind it. I do not disagree on that, and I do not either want to not collaborate with artists. Last, but not least, I need to specify this is not a job offer. As I said, I am looking for partners to start a company with. I won't be the owner, we will be. As a consequence, there's no salary at this stage (neither for myself) because there's no company. I don't know who you are, but I'm looking forward to hear from you. Ciao, Matteo
